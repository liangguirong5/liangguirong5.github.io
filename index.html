About

I am a second-year Ph.D. student working with Ming Li at University of Waterloo .

The focus of my research lies on language modeling and text representation learning.

Before that, I worked on spoken language understanding for task-oriented dialogue system with Yu Zhou, Jiajun Zhang, and Chengqing Zong at University of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.
